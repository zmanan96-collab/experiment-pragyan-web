## SECTION 5 — PROJECTS AND CASE STUDIES

We partner with businesses across manufacturing, education, sustainable goods, and consumer brands.

### 5.1 ATC Chains India (Industrial Manufacturing)
- **Scope**: Brand Strategy, LinkedIn Content Strategy, Exhibition Communication.
- **Context**: 40-year conveyor component manufacturer. Trust was established traditionally, but they needed online credibility as procurement managers increasingly research suppliers online.
- **Solution**: Developed a cohesive brand kit and standard visual system; executed an organic LinkedIn strategy sharing operational expertise; designed exhibition AV materials.
- **Results**: Monthly reach grew from 2,663 to 7,079 impressions over 8 months, building a highly visible B2B brand.

### 5.2 Global Guardians School (Education)
- **Scope**: Brand Strategy, Visual Identity, Admissions Communication, OOH Campaign, Social Media.
- **Context**: Value-based school combining academic excellence with consciousness development. The philosophy was distinctive but unfamiliar to parents.
- **Solution**: Developed visual identity and circular emblem representing harmony; created brochures, stationery, and admissions collateral; ran an OOH billboard campaign across Visnagar.
- **Results**: Unified admissions and public communications under a coherent identity parents understand and trust.

### 5.3 Bhavya Srishti Udyog (Sustainable Manufacturing / Bamboo)
- **Scope**: Brand Strategy, Visual Identity, B2B Collateral, Founder LinkedIn Branding, PR.
- **Context**: Startup expanding the role of bamboo in modern construction. Faced a credibility gap as an emerging category.
- **Solution**: Built visual identity and communication frameworks; created technical reports, presentations, and product assets; managed LinkedIn marketing for company and founder.
- **Results** (April-May 2026 LinkedIn): 12,504 impressions, 7,712 accounts reached, and 56 new followers.

### 5.4 Shreeji Enterprise (Roofing Solutions)
- **Scope**: Visual Identity, Logo Design, Stationery, Brochure Design.
- **Context**: Industrial engineered roofing manufacturer. High quality, but their visual presentation didn't reflect their professionalism.
- **Solution**: Visual identity system representing precision and structure; logo integrating roofing lines and layered construction; standardized corporate folders and quotes.
- **Results**: Enabled sales teams to hold high-confidence client and quoting conversations.

### 5.5 Yash Engineers (India) Pvt Ltd (Metal Processing Machinery)
- **Scope**: Identity Design, Brochure and Technical Collateral Design.
- **Context**: Complex machinery manufacturer. Brochures lacked visual hierarchy and precision.
- **Solution**: Redesigned technical brochures focusing on structured technical data and high readability.
- **Results**: Created high-trust references for technical corporate buyers during long sales cycles.

### 5.6 Bhaktinandan (FMCG / Cold-Pressed Oils)
- **Scope**: Identity Redesign, Packaging System Design, Label Design.
- **Context**: Wooden cold-pressed oil producer needing to stand out in a crowded healthy foods shelf without losing heritage.
- **Solution**: Brand identity and packaging system centered on purity and simplicity.
- **Results**: Established a premium retail presence and unified product labeling.

### 5.7 Ganesh Verma (Personal Branding)
- **Scope**: LinkedIn Profile Optimisation, Content Theme and Thought Leadership Strategy.
- **Context**: Founder of Bhavya Srishti Udyog, needing to build authority in green construction and sustainable materials.
- **Solution**: Structured LinkedIn themes around real green projects, manufacturing insights, and practical builder realities.
