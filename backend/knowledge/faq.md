# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are the real FAQs and business rules for Pragyan.

## 1. What is brand strategy?
Brand strategy is the foundational blueprint that defines who you are, who you serve, and how you communicate. It aligns your business goals with audience needs to create a distinct, long-lasting market position.

## 2. How long does a branding project take?
Most comprehensive branding projects take between **6 to 12 weeks**, depending on the scope of work — ranging from initial research and strategy to full visual identity, packaging, and guideline creation.

## 3. Do you work with startups?
Yes. We frequently partner with early-stage founders to build their initial brand foundations, helping them launch with clarity and professional credibility to attract early adopters and investors. What matters to us is that you care about building something with intent.

## 4. What does personal branding include?
Our personal branding service helps founders and leaders articulate their unique expertise. We develop LinkedIn strategies, personal positioning, and content themes that build trust and industry authority.

## 5. How much do your projects cost?
At Pragyan, we design every engagement from the ground up — based on your industry, your audience, and the specific outcomes you're working toward. There is no standard package, which means pricing is scoped individually for each client. We'd love to understand your needs first. We recommend starting with a discovery call so we can understand your situation and share an honest recommendation.
