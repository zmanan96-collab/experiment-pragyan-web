# Case Studies & Real Work

Pragyan partners with founders and established businesses to build brands with clarity, strategy, and intent. Below are real case studies demonstrating our work across manufacturing, education, sustainability, and consumer goods.

---

## 1. ATC Chains India
- **Industry**: Industrial Manufacturing / Conveyor Components / Food & Beverage
- **Services Provided**: Brand Strategy, LinkedIn Content Strategy, Exhibition & Audiovisual Communication
- **The Challenge**: ATC Chains India has manufactured conveyor components for over four decades, earning trust through product quality and relationships. However, buying behavior was changing—procurement managers increasingly research suppliers online before making contact. ATC's technical expertise was not visible digitally.
- **Our Solution**:
  - Developed a cohesive brand kit establishing consistency across visual expression and customer touchpoints.
  - Crafted an organic LinkedIn content strategy sharing operational knowledge, manufacturing insights, and conveyor component expertise.
  - Designed professional audiovisual (AV) materials for industrial exhibitions and events.
- **Results**:
  - Grew LinkedIn organic reach from 2,663 to 7,079 monthly impressions over 8 months.
  - Established a highly visible, professional B2B presence that builds trust before sales meetings.

## 2. Global Guardians School
- **Industry**: Education
- **Services Provided**: Brand Strategy, Identity & Expression, Content & Communication, Social Media Management, OOH Advertising
- **The Challenge**: Global Guardians School combines academic excellence with consciousness development and value-based holistic education. While the school's philosophy was highly distinctive, it was unfamiliar to parents. The challenge was to communicate this philosophy clearly without reducing it to generic marketing slogans.
- **Our Solution**:
  - Developed a visual identity system including logo, color architecture, typography, and guidelines. The circular emblem represents harmony and universal order.
  - Created a comprehensive admissions communication system (brochures, flyers, forms, and stationery).
  - Executed a strategic out-of-home (OOH) outdoor advertising campaign across Visnagar.
  - Managed social media to showcase everyday learning, values, and school culture.
- **Results**:
  - Created a highly cohesive and recognizable brand across public spaces and admissions material.
  - Helped prospective families understand and engage with the school's unique educational values.

## 3. Bhavya Srishti Udyog (BSU)
- **Industry**: Sustainable Manufacturing / Eco-Conscious / Bamboo
- **Services Provided**: Brand Strategy, Identity & Expression, Content & Communication, Social & Community Management, LinkedIn Marketing, Public Relations
- **The Challenge**: Bhavya Srishti Udyog manufactures innovative structural products utilizing bamboo. Because bamboo is an emerging category in modern construction, the brand faced a credibility gap—needing to educate multiple audiences (architects, builders, developers) and prove reliability.
- **Our Solution**:
  - Built a comprehensive visual identity and strategic communication framework.
  - Developed technical reports, presentations, and product assets supporting business development.
  - Executed a LinkedIn content and personal branding campaign for the company and its leadership to build authority in green construction.
- **Results** (Organic LinkedIn Campaign, April – May 2026):
  - 12,504 impressions and 7,712 accounts reached.
  - 683 link clicks and 56 new followers.
  - Established BSU as a credible pioneer in sustainable construction materials.

## 4. Shreeji Enterprise

- **The Challenge**: Shreeji Enterprise designs and manufactures high-performance roofing solutions for industrial and commercial projects. Their technical execution was stellar, but their visual materials and brand presentation did not match their internal professionalism.

  - Unified all touchpoints under a confident, structured visual system.

## 6. Bhaktinandan
- **Industry**: D2C Food / Consumer Goods / Health & Wellness
- **Services Provided**: Brand Identity Redesign, Packaging System Design, Label Design, Brand Communication
- **The Challenge**: Bhaktinandan produces wooden cold-pressed oils using traditional extraction methods. With the wellness shelf becoming increasingly crowded, claiming "authenticity" was no longer enough; the brand needed packaging that immediately communicated purity and quality.
- **Our Solution**:
  - Redesigned the brand identity, logo, and product packaging labels.
  - Developed a visual language focused on purity and simplicity, utilizing clean modern layouts that remain rooted in traditional heritage.
- **Results**:
  - Created a premium retail shelf presence that stands out and justifies its quality.
  - Unified the product line under a consistent and modern packaging system.
